🏛️ Stamp Duties
BSD (Buyer's Stamp Duty) — Tiered rates on purchase price:
1% on first S$180k · 2% on next S$180k · 3% on next S$640k · 4% on next S$500k · 5% on remainder

🏛️ ABSD Rates (effective 27 Apr 2023)
Singapore Citizen: 0% (1st) · 20% (2nd) · 30% (3rd+)
Singapore PR: 5% (1st) · 30% (2nd) · 35% (3rd+)
Foreigner: 60% (all) · Entity: 65% (all)

🏦 LTV & Loan Rules
Max LTV 75% for bank loans (1st property, no outstanding loans)
Minimum cash downpayment: 5% of purchase price
Remaining 20% can be CPF OA and/or cash
Max loan tenure: 30 years (or up to age 65, whichever is shorter)

🧮 Mortgage Calculation
Standard reducing-balance amortisation formula
Two-phase: fixed rate (years 1–N), then SORA + spread
Phase 2 payment computed on actual remaining balance at switch date
Monthly payment = P × [r(1+r)ⁿ] / [(1+r)ⁿ−1]
where r = annual rate ÷ 12, n = remaining months
